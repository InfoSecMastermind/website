Amdad

Amdad is a community-driven platform dedicated to connecting individuals in need with a wide array of free or affordable resources. Our mission is to bridge the gap between valuable services and support provided by local organizations, NGOs, and businesses and the individuals who can benefit from them the most. Amdad relies on verified information, community collaboration, and continuous improvement to ensure that everyone has easy access to essential support in times of financial challenges or life transitions. Join Amdad, and let's build a stronger, more connected community together.

VoteMerit

VoteMerit introduces a data-driven approach to evaluating and comparing politicians. Using evidence-based facts, the platform scores leaders on performance metrics such as achievements, policy consistency, education, and experience. This objective scoring system enables the ranking of politicians, providing voters with a clear and transparent view of their capabilities. With VoteMerit, voters can make informed decisions, comparing and selecting leaders based on tangible and impactful criteria.

CensorX

CensorX is a user-friendly and lightweight application that assists people in avoiding unwanted content. Using smart technology, it quickly detects and censors inappropriate visuals, audio, or text. Tailored to individual preferences, CensorX provides a customizable solution for those looking to manage exposure to explicit material.

FoodGrader

Food Grader is here to make your food experiences awesome! Imagine having a friendly guide that not only helps you find the tastiest dishes but also lets you share your favorite food finds with others. It's like having a food-loving buddy who understands your cravings, recommends personalized delights, and makes exploring local gems and seasonal specials a delightful journey. Join Food Grader, your go-to companion for discovering and rating dishes that match your unique tastes!
